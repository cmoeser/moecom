Directions To My Studio

43-01 22nd St.
4th Floor Studio 440
Long Island City, NY 11101

- SUBWAY

Closest stations: Ely/23rd St., 45th Rd/Court house Sq., Queens Plaza, Queensboro Plaza, 21st St. Queens Bridge

E or V to 23rd St./Ely Ave.: Take exit to 21st street and 44th Dr. Turn right on 21st Street. Walk two blocks and turn right on 44th Ave. Turn left on 22nd Street. (2.5 blocks)

G to Court Square: Exit to 44th Drive. Turn left on 44th Dr to 23rd Street, turn right on 23rd St. towards 44th Ave. Turn left on 44th Ave. and right on 22nd Street. (2.5 blocks)

7 to 45th Road/Courthouse Sq.: You will exit on 23rd St. Walk north towards 44th Ave. Turn left on 44th Ave and right on 22nd street. (3 blocks)

N or W to Queensboro Plaza.: Look for 27th St or Crescent. Walk 2 blocks south towards 43rd Ave. Right on 43rd Ave and left on 22nd St. (4 blocks)

F to 21st Street: On 21st Street, go under Queensboro Bridge to 43rd Ave. Left on 43rd Ave and right on 22nd St. (3.5 blocks)

R to Queens Plaza.: Take south on Jackson to 43rd Ave. Right on 43rd Ave to 22nd St. Left on 22nd St. (7 blocks)

- TRAIN

Long Island Railroad to Woodside, switch to #7 Subway train to 45th Rd/Courthouse Square.

MetroNorth to Grand Central Station, switch to #7 Subway train to 45th Rd/Courthouse Square.

New Jersey trains to Penn Station,#1, 9, 2, or 3 trains to Times Square, switch to #7 Subway train to 45th Rd/Courthouse Square.

- CAR

From Manhattan: 59th Street Bridge, Upper Roadway, 21st Street Exit... Turn left on 21st Street. Left on 43rd Ave and right on 22nd Street. If you take Lower level, exit on Queens Blvd and turn right on Jackson Ave. Drive south 3 blocks and turn Right on 43rd Ave to 22nd Street. Legal parking is usually available on some of the streets and avenues. Parking is also available on 43rd Ave, 24th St and on 23rd St.

Long Island Expressway: Take exit Van Dam St. Turn right on Van Dam and left on Thompson Ave. Cross the elevated and do not turn to Manhattan. Keep on right to Jackson Ave. Turn right on Jackson Ave and immediately turn left on 43rd Ave. Follow to left turn on 22nd St.

Grand Central Parkway: Exit on Hoyt Ave, keep right and go to 21st St. Turn left on 21st St and go approximately 2 miles. Pass under Queensboro Bridge to 43rd Ave. Turn left on 43rd and turn right on 22nd St.

Bronx, Westchester, Connecticut: Whitestone Bridge to Whitestone Expressway towards Manhattan. Switch to Grand Central Parkway. See directions above for Grand Central Parkway.

Neck Bridge: Take Cross Island Expressway to Whitestone Expressway to Grand Central Parkway. See directions above for Grand Central Parkway.

Please contact MTA for Bus Directions.
